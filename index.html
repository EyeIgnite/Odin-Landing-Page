Bleach

Bleach is a manga/anime written by Tite Kubo. It revolves around a high-school student who can see ghosts named Ichigo Kurosaki. During an attack on his family by a ghostly monster known as a hollow, he meets a Soul Reaper named Rukia Kuchiki, who is injured during the encounter. Rukia then grants Ichigo her Soul Reaper powers in order to save his family.
